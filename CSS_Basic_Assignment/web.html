Alopecia Awareness

Alopecia is an autoimmune condition that causes hair loss on the scalp and body. It affects millions of people worldwide, but awareness and support can make a difference.

What Causes Alopecia?

Alopecia occurs when the immune system mistakenly attacks hair follicles, causing hair loss. It can be triggered by genetics, stress, or environmental factors.

Let's Raise Awareness!

People with alopecia deserve understanding, acceptance, and support. Help spread awareness by sharing this information.

Together, we can break the stigma around hair loss!

You Are Not Alone

Alopecia does not define your beauty. Join support groups and connect with others who understand.
